About Us

MISSION STATEMENT

Bury Defence Academy is a martial arts and boxing club,
"Run by the Community for the community".
Our goal is to help develop the potential of the people of Bury, whilst being able to give back to our community.We also work with organisations and sponsors to improve wellbeing and to educate the young people in our community.
We are currently working with a partner organisation to divert young people away from crime.Our main goal as a charitable institution, is to recycle back profits into our gym community and the community of Bury.
Our journey hasn't been easy but we would like to thank each and every person who has supported us on this journey"
