Our Facilities

Here are some facilities that are available at our gym

Boxing Ring

Our gym has two boxing rings dedicated to sparring.
These can be used at anytime when the gym is open &
boxing equipment is also free to use.

Martial Arts Area

A dedicated mat area mainly used for martial arts & wrestling.
Sambo and BJJ classes are also held here, For more
information select the classes page.

Equipment

There is a section within the gym where equipment is available.
Machines such as Treadmills, Bikes, Crosstrainers & more also widely accessible.
